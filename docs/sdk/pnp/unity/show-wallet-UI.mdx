To top up the wallet of a user, you can use the `launchWalletServices(ChainConfig)` function. This
method takes in a ChainConfig parameter to configure the blockchain network. On clicking, a browser
will appear with the information of your tokens, NFTs, all the activity from the wallet, connect
your wallet through WalletConnect and the possibility to send, receiver or buy tokens.
